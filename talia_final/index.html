Mermaid Party

By: Talia Duran

This webcomics tells the story of Selena and Padame - two mermaid friends - and the party they decide to attend. This page introduces the main characters and the first few panels, created in Procreate.

About Selena: Selena is around 100 years old. She is very oblivious to her surroundings, loves partying with her friends!

About Padame: Padame is around 121 years old. She is a trendy mermaid with a love for fashion. She cares deeply about her friend Selena.
